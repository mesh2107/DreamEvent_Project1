About us

Welcome To DreamEvent

DreamEvent was established under the parent company WT Technologies in 2017 with the aim of providing technological solutions to the event industry. We want to transform the event experience of people by making sure that every event, no matter the scale, small or large, as well as organizations of all sizes, have access to our platform.

DreamEvent is for individuals organizing events, organizations the weddings and Private event.

Our Story

With the need for end-to-end event tech solutions, DreamEvent was born. We already provided design and technological solutions like delivering personalized invites and videos on WhatsApp. By introducing DreamEvent, we further took a step ahead in providing technological solutions for events: QR Code invites, E-ticketing solution, and a lot more…

DreamEvent never stops to add innovative tech solutions to its product portfolio. White-label solution, API integrations, and much more have been added during the past years

Our Mission

Our mission is to provide design and technological solutions for the event industry. From sending QR Code invitations to getting real-time check-in updates, DreamEvent ensures your event runs smoothly from start to finish. Get all the technology for your events at your fingertips with DreamEvent.

Our Vision

Transforming the way people connect and celebrate the events. DreamEvent is more than just an event technology platform—it’s your digital event partner. Whether you’re planning a wedding, a corporate conference, or a mega cultural event, our cutting-edge services are built to simplify every step. We aim to redefine how events are planned and managed.
